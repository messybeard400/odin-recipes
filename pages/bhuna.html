Chicken Bhuna

Ingredients

  • 3 medium onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 minced fresh ginger
  • chicken breast
  • 2 medium chopped tomatoes
  • chilli powder, ground cumin, ground turmeric

Directions

  1. Add onion to hot pan and cook till soft
  2. Reduce the heat to low, stir in garlic and ginger
  3. Add chilli powder, ground cumin, ground turmeric. Stir in tomatoes for 5 mins.
  4. Add chicken and cook until brown for 5-7 minutes

Enjoy!
