Salmon Salad Rice Bowl

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped cucumber
  • soy sauce
  • 1 can pink salmon, skin and bone removed
  • light mayonnaise
  • 1 cup cooked white rice
  • 1 avocado cut and peeled
  • 1 tablespoon green onion and garnish

Directions

  1. Combine chopped cucumbers and soy sauce in small bowl.
  2. In another small bowl, combine salmon, mayonnaise and rice vinegar.
  3. In plating bowl, put cooked rice, and top it with the salmon mixture, sliced avocado and cucumber mixture.
  4. Add green onion and garnish on top.

Enjoy!
