Chicken and Brocoli Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccine
  • 2 cups brocoli
  • 1 lb chicken breast
  • 1 cups milk
  • 4 ounces cream cheese

Directions

  1. Cook pasta as directed on package.
  2. Add broccoli to boiling water for the last 2 minutes of past cooking
  3. Cook chicken in large non-stick skillet on med-high heat
  4. Stir in milk, cream cheese, and chicken with pasta.
  5. Cook until sauce is stirred and blended

Enjoy!
